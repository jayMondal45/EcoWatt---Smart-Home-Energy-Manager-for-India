Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the savings estimates?

Our estimates are based on Indian energy consumption patterns and real-world testing. Individual results may vary based on your specific usage patterns and state electricity rates.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and we never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. We comply with Indian data protection regulations.

Do I need to buy any hardware?

No hardware required! Our system works by analyzing the devices you already own. We provide recommendations based on your current setup and usage patterns.

How often should I update my device list?

We recommend reviewing your devices every 3-6 months, or whenever you add new appliances or change your usage patterns significantly.

Do you provide information about government subsidies?

Yes! We provide updated information about central and state government subsidies for solar panels, energy-efficient appliances, and other green initiatives.

Can I use this for my business?

Currently we focus on residential energy management. However, many of our tips and recommendations apply to small businesses and offices as well.
