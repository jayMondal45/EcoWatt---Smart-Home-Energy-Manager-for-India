Top Energy Saving Tips for Indian Homes

Simple changes that make a big difference

Use Ceiling Fans with AC

Save ₹1,000/month

Set your AC temperature 2-3°C higher and use ceiling fans. This can reduce AC electricity consumption by 25-30%.

Switch to 5-Star Appliances

Save ₹600/month

BEE 5-star rated appliances consume 30-50% less electricity. Look for the star rating when buying new appliances.

Solar Water Heating

Save ₹1,200/month

Install solar water heaters to reduce your water heating electricity costs by up to 70%. Government subsidies available.

LED Lighting

Save ₹500/month

Replace all incandescent and CFL bulbs with LED bulbs. They use 75% less energy and last much longer.

Smart Power Management

Save ₹250/month

Use smart plugs to eliminate phantom loads from TVs, set-top boxes, and chargers when not in use.

Regular Maintenance

Save ₹400/month

Clean AC filters monthly and service appliances regularly. Well-maintained appliances work more efficiently.
